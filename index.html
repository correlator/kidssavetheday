Entertain Kids, Create Jobs.

With the outbreak of Covid-19 there are a lot of children staying home and parents looking for non-screen based intertainment. We are selling boxes of 7 experiments along with instructions that guide you through the steps and science required for you and your child to learn something about the universe together.

BackStory

We really owe this to my wife. With our first baby on the way, Jessie put together a collection of science experiments to do with our child as they learned about the universe. In a previous life I was a PhD physics candidate at Yale University, so when she shared these experimnets with me, I dug in and started putting together a learning carriculum for our future child.

Giving Back

Create Jobs

For every 500 kits we sell, we will employ someone who lost thier job due to the virus outbreak to construct and send out kits. We will have a video call with them every day where we talk for 5 minutes, while they take thier temperature on camera and we listen for any coughs or signs of sickness.

Share Expereinces

We will be donating 1 kit for every 5 we sell to a family in need. In addition, you can opt to purchase kits for those in need at a 20% discount. We will distribute the kit to those in need on a first come first serve basis.
